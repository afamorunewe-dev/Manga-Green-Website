BORUTO: TWO BLUE VORTEX

Boruto: Two Blue Vortex is the sequel to the Boruto series, following the adventures of Boruto Uzumaki and his friends as they face new challenges and threats in the ninja world. After Eida changes the minds of the villagers, making them think Boruto killed Naruto, Sarada, who was unaffected by the jutsu, makes Sasuke realize that Boruto didn't kill Naruto, after she unlocks her Mangekyou Sharingan right in front of him, making him gain the trust of Boruto. How will Boruto regain the trust of the Hidden Leaf, and make everyone know that it was Kawaki who trapped Naruto and Hinata in another dimension (called Daikokuten), and more importantly, handle the new threats of Code's Thorn Bulb creations, mainly the threats of Jura? New battles await in the ninja world.
