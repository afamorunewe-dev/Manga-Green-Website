JUJUTSU KAISEN

In a reality where negative human emotions give birth to terrifying beings called Curses, secret sorcerers fight back using a dangerous art known as Jujutsu with their source of energy known as Cursed Energy. Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, an ordinary student who gets pulled into this brutal hidden world after making a life-changing decision—one that ties his fate to the most feared Curse of all. Expect intense battles, dark themes, unforgettable characters, and a story that doesn’t hold your hand. This is a series where power comes at a cost, survival is never guaranteed, and every fight matters.
